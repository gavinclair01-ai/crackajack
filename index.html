What drill does it work with?

Any cordless drill with a 19mm socket — the same setup you already use on your stability legs. If it turns a 19mm socket, it'll turn the Cracka Jack.

Does it fit my caravan?

Cracka Jack fits any standard 50mm A-frame jockey wheel clamp. If your current jockey wheel fits a 50mm clamp, Cracka Jack will drop straight in with no modification needed.
